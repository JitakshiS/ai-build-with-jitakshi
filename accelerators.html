In early 2026, Volta Foundation partnered with me to run Atlantic Canada's first women-focused AI build sprint. Every participant shipped a working product. Here's what that looked like.

The format

  • 4 weekly hands-on sessions (3 hours each)
  • Office hours and 1:1 support between sessions
  • Tools: Lovable, Claude Code, Vercel
  • Demo day with live presentations
  • Zero coding background required
